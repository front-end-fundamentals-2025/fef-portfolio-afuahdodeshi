The Art Market in Jakande is a unique gathering space where
artisans bring their creativity to life in ways that are both refreshing and rare.
Here, you’ll find carefully crafted jewelry, handmade bags, custom clothing, sculptures,
and expressive paintings, each reflecting the individual touch of its creator.
What sets these artists apart is their dedication to sourcing materials themselvesstones from the northern caves,
leather from local cattle, and other raw elements that add an authentic richness to each piece.
Tucked away from the city’s main routes, the Art Market is a hidden gem that deserves a wider spotlight,
offering a glimpse into the untapped creative talents of Jakande.
